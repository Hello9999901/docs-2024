# Carrier Board

## CM4

- HDMI
- USB 2.0
- Ethernet (w/ POE)
- Hard power on and reset

## Sensors/IC

- PCA9685 (pwm)
- BME680 (gas)
- MMC5603NJ (magneto)
- BMI088 (accel/gyro)
- MS5837 (depth)
- Neopixel (debug/status)
- Piezo Buzzer (debug/status)
- Screen (debug/status)
- Light strip (debug/status)

## Other ICs

- Ethernet Switch
